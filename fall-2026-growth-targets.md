## Outreach Angle by Tier

**Tier 1:** Lead with their funding news — "saw you just closed your Series C, congrats — I build growth systems for exactly this stage."

**Tier 2:** Focus on their growth problem — small team, new capital, need to scale pipeline fast. Offer a live demo of your outreach tool.

**Tier 3:** Slightly older capital but still actively building. Good backup pipeline — don't lead here, but keep warm.

---

## Roles to Target at Each Company

- **Growth Associate / Growth Analyst** (most common at Series A/B)
- **Revenue Operations** (Series B/C, 101–250 employees)
- **GTM Strategy** (Series C, larger teams)
- **Business Development** (any tier — easiest cold entry point)
- **Growth Marketing** (companies with clear B2C-ish motion: Humanly, Netomi, Cents)
